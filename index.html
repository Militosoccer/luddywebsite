I Play Football

Being a footballer, I am in the process of breaking into the first team of my club Maryland Bobcats Football Club.

  Alexandria Soccer

At Alexandria Soccer Association I played for the junior academy in which, I focused on four key areas: first touch on the ball, dribbling, ball manipulation and tactical awareness..

  Barca Academy

At Barca Academy I started developing my love for the game more and more. In late summer of 2016 I was chosen to travel to Spain to play for FCBEscola (FC.Barcelona soccer school).

  NPC FF

At NPC FF I was able to start to show off my talents and what I was capable of. I also went on an international tour in France and trained with Olympique Lyonais coach Joel Frechet.

  MD Bobcats

At MD Bobcats where I am now the dream is starting to come true I have begun training with the Bobcats pro team, and I was also selected to represent and play for the U.S Futsal National Team in Portugal.
